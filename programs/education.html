CHILDREN's WELFARE

GSS is committed to ensuring that every child has the opportunity to grow, learn, and thrive in a safe and supportive environment. Our child-focused programs promote nutrition, basic literacy, school readiness, and educational continuity, with a special emphasis on supporting girls’ education.

We have assisted around 250 financially challenged rural students by providing free notebooks, educational materials, and scholarship support, helping reduce school dropouts and improve academic performance. Leadership development and life-skills education further equip children to become confident and responsible citizens.
