HEALTHCARE SERVICES & HYGIENE PROMOTION

GSS works to improve community health by organizing health camps, awareness sessions, and nutritional programs for mothers, adolescent girls, malnourished children, and individuals affected by HIV/AIDS.

Our health initiatives address Reproductive and Child Health (RCH), sexually transmitted diseases (STDs), HIV/AIDS, nutrition, and general health awareness. So far, we have reached 600 rural women and community members, enabling better access to healthcare information and services.

In parallel, GSS promotes personal hygiene practices through regular campaigns and orientations, contributing to disease prevention and improved overall well-being.
