WHO WE ARE

Gandhiji Seva Sangam (GSS) is a grassroots non-governmental organisation founded in 1992 by Mr. K. Pitchai. For over three decades, GSS has uplifted rural communities through women's empowerment, child development, youth training, health education, and sustainable livelihood programs. Rooted in Gandhian values, we serve marginalized families across Tamil Nadu.

Programs / What We Do

Women Empowerment

Empowering women through self-help groups, skill training, and financial literacy programs.

Livelihood Enhancement

Supporting sustainable income generation through vocational training and microenterprises.

Children's Welfare

Providing education, nutrition, and developmental support for children's holistic growth.

Youth Development

Building leadership skills and career opportunities for rural youth through training programs.

Healthcare Services

Delivering health education, medical camps, and wellness programs for community health.

Environment Protection

Promoting sustainable practices through tree plantation and environmental awareness initiatives.
